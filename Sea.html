Marine life, also known as sea life, or ocean life, is made up of the plants, animals and other organisms that live in the salty water of the seas and oceans of Earth. More than 200,000 marine species have been documented, and perhaps two million marine species are yet to be documented.

Marine life can range in size from the microscopic phytoplankton (which can be as small as 0.02 micrometres), to the huge cetaceans such as the blue whale.
